About Us

At 518 K-9 Solutions, we believe that every dog has the potential to be a well-behaved, confident, and happy companion. Our professional training programs are designed to strengthen the bond between you and your dog while building essential skills through proven, humane methods.

We understand that each dog is unique, which is why we create customized training plans tailored to your dog's specific needs, temperament, and your family's lifestyle. Whether you're starting with basic obedience training, looking to advance your dog's skills, or seeking specialized training, we're here to help.

Our commitment extends beyond training sessions. We provide ongoing support and guidance to ensure long-lasting results and a harmonious relationship between you and your four-legged family member.

Sai Hawk

Owner & Lead Trainer

With years of experience in canine behavior and training, Sai brings expertise, patience, and genuine love for dogs to every training session. Certified in multiple training methodologies, Sai is dedicated to helping dogs and their families achieve their training goals.

Our Services

Basic Obedience Training

Foundation commands including sit, stay, come, down, and heel. Perfect for puppies and adult dogs learning essential manners and building a strong training foundation.

Advanced Obedience Training

Build upon basic commands with advanced training techniques including off-leash control, distance commands, and complex task sequences for well-trained companions.

Puppy Training Programs

Specialized programs for puppies focusing on socialization, house training, bite inhibition, and establishing good habits during critical developmental periods.

Specialized Training

Custom training programs designed for specific needs including therapy dog preparation, service dog foundation skills, and specialized working dog training.

Private Training Sessions

One-on-one training sessions in your home or our facility, providing personalized attention and customized training plans for optimal results.

Group Classes

Structured group training sessions that provide socialization opportunities while learning essential skills in a controlled, supportive environment.
